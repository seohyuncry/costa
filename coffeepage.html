Sourcing our coffee beans sustainably

Coffee is at the absolute core of our business and we believe that great coffee has the power to create positive change. That's why we take great pride in sourcing it sustainably.

We make careful choices about the coffee we buy, working with the Rainforest Alliance to source 100% of our famous beans to their high standard. We're also exploring new ways to partner with farmers to tackle the effects of climate change and support their livelihoods.

Our progress and promises

  • Now

    About 100 percent of Costa Coffee's beans in the U.K. are certified by the Rainforest Alliance. Costa coffee beans make each bean perfectly the same, and you can feel your senses awake just by the scent. If you want a cup of strong and fresh coffee, please visit Costa.

  • Near

    We plan to collaborate with coffee experts to carry out projects that focus on realizing more sustainable coffee farming methods. As such, Costa Coffee is ncreasingly striving to present the best drinks and desserts to our customers. Meet the more delicious Costa coffee.

  • Next

    We will evaluate the impact of the business on nature and set specific goals to reduce it. The business is accomplished by our iconic signature blend, which is a perfect combination and balance of delicate Arabica beans and strong Robusta beans.

Our work with the Rainforest Alliance

We ensure that 100% of our coffee beans come from Rainforest Alliance Certified farms. This means that for every cup of coffee we serve, we invest back into the coffee-growing communities we depend on.

At present, due to global supply chain disruptions, we are temporarily unable to source enough certified coffee with the Rainforest Alliance seal to maintain 100% coverage in our stores, Costa Express, and Ready to Drink and At Home ranges. These products continue to contain at least 70% Rainforest Alliance Certified coffee ingredients.

We'll return to sourcing all our coffee from Rainforest Alliance Certified farms as soon as these disruptions ease. Find out more here.

A better future for nature, people and businesses

The Rainforest Alliance is an international non-profit organisation, working to create a better future for people and nature by making responsible business the new normal. By working together with farmers, businesses and consumers, its aim is to protect forests, improve the livelihoods of farmers and farming communities, and help them adapt to climate change.

The Rainforest Alliance, and its little green frog seal, is recognised by companies and consumers around the world as a symbol of environmental, social and economic sustainability.

Rainforest Alliance certification makes a huge impact across a whole range of different areas. These can be split up into three sections: people, planet and profit.

  • People

    Rainforest Alliance Certified Farms are better and safer workplaces where human rights should be respected. The Forest Alliance here is an international non-profit organization that focuses on business, agriculture, and forests. This helps make ethical and responsible business practices a new standard.

  • Planet

    Farmers are trained to use land, water, and energy very carefully so that these important resources do not go to waste. That's why Costa's coffee is eco-friendly. It guarantees that 100% of the coffee beans come from a rainforest alliance certified farm. This means Costa Coffee is investing again in coffee-growing communities.

  • Profit

    Rainforest Union farming methods lead to better and larger crops while lowering production costs. This is more money for our fantastic farmers! The benefits of farming like this help farmers produce better crops, adapt to climate change, increase productivity, and reduce costs. These benefits enable businesses to deliver certified products consistently and securely.

Celebrating our partnership with the Rainforest Alliance

In September 2021, we celebrated 13 years of partnership with the Rainforest Alliance, working in collaboration to ensure that delivering great coffee supports the best possible future for people and nature.

This partnership means we were the first UK coffee shop to source 100% of our coffee from Rainforest Alliance Certified farms helping to ensure that the coffee we serve in all our drinks has been produced more responsibly and sustainably. Learn more about our work with the Rainforest Alliance by watching our 'Follow the frog' video.

So, this means you can be confident that you’re supporting the farmers and forest communities who are looking after our natural resources whenever you choose to drink Costa Coffee.
