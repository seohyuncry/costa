How to order for delivery

  • Place your order with our partners

    Simple way to get your favorite Costa coffee. Freshly made coffee, delicious meals, and delicious snacks are prepared directly in the store and delivered directly. Start by downloading or visiting the Uber Eats, Deliveryoo, or Just Eat app or website.

  • Search for Costa Coffee and browse

    Set the location and select Favorite from the Available menu. Find your preferred shipping partner and download it to iOS and Android to start ordering.

  • Sit back and relax. Your order is on the way

    Our baristas will arrange for contactless pickup and delivery of your order in the store. So sit back and relax. The product you ordered is being delivered.
