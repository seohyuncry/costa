British Smoked Bacon Bap

British Smoked Bacon Bap

Soft white foam and sticky back bacon. It is especially famous for the bacon that British people enjoy eating.

Ingredients

White Roll (Wheat Flour (WHEAT Flour, Calcium Carbonate, Iron, Niacin, Thiamin), Water, Durum WHEAT Semolina, Sugar, Rapeseed Oil, Yeast, Salt, WHEAT Flour, Broad Bean Flour, Sourdough (Water, Fermented WHEAT Flour), WHEAT Gluten, Flour Treatment Agent (Ascorbic Acid)), Beechwood Smoked Back Bacon (23%) (Pork, Salt, Preservative (Sodium Nitrite)), Beechwood Smoked Streaky Bacon (6%) (Pork, Salt, Antioxidant (E301), Preservative (Sodium Nitrite)), Unsalted Butter (MILK).

! Adults need around 2000 kcal a day
