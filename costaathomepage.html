Coffee at home the Costa way

Our wide range of award winning* at home products offer the same iconic Signature Blend taste you'd expect to find in our stores, but to be enjoyed at home, and in your favourite mug. There's something for everyone to suit a variety of brewing methods for you to prepare your coffee just how you like it in the comfort of your own home. Discover and shop our range here.

  • Roast & Ground

    To create your perfect coffee experience at home, use a heaped tablespoon of our fresh, rich-tasting Roast & Ground coffee in a cafetière or filter machine and allow to brew for 3-5 minutes before serving. Then all you need to do is sit back, relax and enjoy. Available in our Signature Blend and a range of single origin roasts.

    Shop now >

  • Nespresso Compatible Pods

    Enjoy our intense Signature Blend and a range of single origin roasts at home with Nespresso® compatible pods. Simply place your mug under the machine, pop in one of the capsules and select the correct setting for the perfect cup of coffee - every time.   • Dolce Gusto Compatible Pods

    Enjoy our indulgent Signature Blend as your favourite coffee shop drink at home with Dolce Gusto® compatible pods. They’re easy to use and pack all the flavour and taste that you expect from Costa Coffee.     Shop now >

  • Premium Instant

    We’ve created this Premium Instant coffee with the recognisable smooth and nutty taste of our Signature Blend, mixing finely ground roasted beans with instant coffee for the perfect pick-me-up. Plus, our Smooth Medium Roast Premium Instant won Product of the Year 2021.* You can change your choices at any time by visiting Cookie Preferences, as described in the Cookie Notice.

    Shop how >

  • Whole Bean

    What could be more rewarding than grinding your own coffee beans at home? Our fresh whole coffee beans are available in our Signature Blend and can be enjoyed at home using a bean-to-cup coffee machine to make the perfect cup of coffee. This Costa Signature Blend is certified by the Rainforest Alliance, ensuring sustainable farming to encourage sustainable livelihoods by transforming consumer behaviour
