recycle

Recycle your Costa Coffee pods in three easy steps

  • Step 1: Pick up a bag

    Ask the barista at the Costa Coffee store for a Ford recycling bag. Here, coffee pod recycling means that recycled aluminum pods are used to manufacture beverage cans and auto parts.

  • Step 2: Fill it up

    Each bag holds up to 75 pods of plastic or aluminum. Each bag, known as a coffee bag, is reusable. Not only can you save money, but you can also keep reusing it once you have enough plants to plant in your garden.

  • Step 3: Drop it off

    Seal the bag and leave it at our Costa store. I'll sort it out and send it to you for recycling! For coffee pods that are classified and recycled, capsules and coffee grounds can be reused in new and innovative ways to make fertilizers, cars, bicycles, computers, and more.
