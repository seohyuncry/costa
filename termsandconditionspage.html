Reusable cups - What to know when using a reusable cup in our stores

* We accept any reusable cup, any size, whether it's Costa branded or your own - we're not fussy.

* When your barista takes your order, remove the lid of your reusable cup and keep hold of it whilst you hand them the rest of your cup.

* We always welcome clean cups, but don't worry if it's not completely empty, the team will be happy to rinse your cup should you need them to do so.

* When your drink is ready, your barista will sit your cup on the counter for you to place your lid back on your cup to take away and enjoy.

* Also, if you're a member of our Costa Club loyalty scheme, every time you buy a barista made drink (Hot or Cold) in a reusable cup we’ll give you an extra Bean.
