Coronation Toastie

Coronation Toastie

Roast chicken with coronation spiced bechamel sauce, mango chutney, apricots & sultanas in coronation spiced cheese topped bread.

Ingredients

White Bloomer Bread (Wheat Flour (WHEAT Flour, Calcium Carbonate, Iron, Niacin, Thiamin), Water, Yeast, Rapeseed Oil, Salt, Spirit Vinegar, WHEAT Protein, Dextrose, Emulsifiers (E471 and E472e), Palm Oil, Palm Fat, Flour Treatment Agent (Ascorbic Acid), WHEAT Flour), Roast Chicken Breast (20%) (Chicken Breast, Cornflour, Salt), Béchamel Sauce (MILK, Water, Single Cream (MILK), Salted Butter (Butter (MILK), Salt), Wheat Flour (WHEAT Flour, Calcium Carbonate, Iron, Niacin, Thiamin), Cornflour, Salt, White Pepper, Ground Bay Leaf), Mozzarella Cheese (MILK), Mango Chutney (Mango, Sugar, Water, Diced Apple, Spirit Vinegar, Modified Maize Starch, Concentrated Lemon Juice, Red Chilli Purée, Chilli Flakes), Mature Cheddar Cheese (MILK), Coronation Style Paste (Sugar, Rapese- ed Oil, Water, Glucose Syrup, Onion Juice, Salt, White Wine Vinegar, Ground Cumin, Garlic Powder, Ground Coriander, Onion Powder, Ground Turmeric, Ground Fenugreek, Acidity Regulators (Acetic Acid, Citric Acid), Cayenne Pepper, Ground Bay Leaf), Dried Apricots (Apricot, Rice Flour, Preservative (SULPHUR DIOXIDE)), Sultanas, Spiced Breadcrumbs (Wheat Flour (WHEAT Flour, Calcium Carbonate, Iron, Niacin, Thiamin), Onion Powder, Crushed Chilli, Coriander Leaf, Dried Garlic, Salt, Cracked Black Pepper, Ground Cumin, Ground Turmeric, Ground Ginger, Coriander Seeds, Nigella Seeds, Rapeseed Oil, Yeast), Potato Starch.

! Adults need around 2000 kcal a day

Meaning of Food

What does corona- tion mean in food?

An Indian-inspired chicken dish that was served during the celebrations of Elizabeth II's 1953 coronation and created by writer Constance Spry and the founder of Le Cordon Bleu culinary school Rosemary Hume.
