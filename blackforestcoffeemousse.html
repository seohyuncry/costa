From our home to yours

Black Forest Coffee Mousse

Method

1. Add 125ml of boiling water to the instant coffee and stir until combined.

2. Heat the chocolate and coffee in a glass bowl over a pan of boiling water until the chocolate is fully melted.

3. Add the 3 egg yolks to the melted chocolate and coffee mixture and stir until combined.

4. Use a stand mixer or electric hand mixer to beat the 3 egg whites and caster sugar until stiff peaks form then fold through the melted chocolate.

5. When fully combined divide the mixture between 6 glasses and place in the fridge for at least 2 hours until set.

6. To make the cherry compote halve the cherries and remove the stones then place in a saucepan with the caster sugar and orange juice and zest.

7. Bring the mixture to a boil then leave to simmer for 15 minutes until syrupy then leave to cool.

8. Top the chocolate mousse with the cherry compote then a dollop of the whipped cream and garnish each with a cherry and orange zest.

9. When ready to serve whip the cream with the vanilla paste and caster sugar.
