Triple Chocolate Biscuits

Melt in your mouth indulgent all butter biscuits with white, dark & milk chocolate chips.

Ingredients

Wheat Flour (WHEAT Flour, Calcium Carbonate, Iron, Niacin, Thiamin), Salted Butter (23%) (MILK, Salt), White Chocolate Chips (14%) (Sugar, Whole MILK Powder, Cocoa Butter, Skimmed MILK Powder, Emulsifier: SOYA Lecithin; Natural Vanilla Flavouring), Sugar, Dark Chocolate Chips (8%) (Cocoa Mass, Sugar, Dextrose, Emulsifier: SOYA Lecithin), Fat Redu- ced Cocoa Powder (6%), Invert Sugar Syrup, Milk Chocolate Chips (4%) (Sugar, Whole MILK Powder, Cocoa Butter, Cocoa Mass, Emulsifier: SOYA Lecithin; Natural Vanilla Flavouring), Raising Agents: E500(ii), E501(ii); Green Tea Extract, Natural Flavouring.

! Adults need around 2000 kcal a day

Allergen Information

Meaning of Food

Why is it called triple chocolate?

These Triple Chocolate Cookies are: VERY chocolatey – with 3 chocolate hits in the form of cocoa for the cookie dough, dark AND white chocolate chips.
