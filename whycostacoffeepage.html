Why partner with Costa Coffee?

Costa Coffee has been voted the ‘Nation’s Favourite coffee shop*’ 10 years in a row. With 50% of the out of home coffee market spending money at Costa Coffee** and over 6m active Coffee Club users, a partnership with Costa Coffee connects you to our passionate and loyal customer base.

In a UK market that drinks forty four million cups** of espresso based coffee per week, there is opportunity to drive incremental revenue with a great quality coffee offer, whether you operate in food led pubs, restaurants, hotels, healthcare, travel and roadside, education or business and industry.

When you partner with us, you get

  • High consumer reach

    Costa has more than 20 million consumers, which is 50% of the total household coffee consumer base. It is famous for being a favorite coffee of Britons, with Costa Coffee topping the list as the nation's favorite coffee shop in an official survey.

  • Loyalty programme

    Costa has over 6 million Costa Club members and a strong digital transformation plan. Last year, Costa Express launched the world's first integrated self-service coffee machine, the Hot and Ice Beverage Machine, with an additional 1,325 machines installed and more than 500 barista-quality hot and iced drinks.

  • Powerful brand

    We gain awareness, brand love, and brand preference on the streets. Costa's main target customers are men and women, ages 18 to 45, who tell target consumers that drinking Costa coffee can be a more enjoyable and valuable experience than other brands of coffee.

We were the first UK coffee shop chain to commit to sourcing 100% of our coffee and cocoa from Rainforest Alliance Certified farms
