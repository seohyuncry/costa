Wiltshire Ham, Cheddar & Pickle Sandwich

Wiltshire ham with mature Cheddar cheese and sweet pickle on soft malted bread.

Menu that go well with food

What cheese goes best with a ham sandwich?

So many different cheeses work well with ham, but cheddar is the most classic option for a ham and cheese sandwich. I recommend a mature cheddar, just to pack in the flavour. Its sharpness will also help balance out the saltiness and sweetness of the ham.
