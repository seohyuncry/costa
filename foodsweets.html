costa sweets

Costa has as many sweet desserts as delicious drinks! Smaller than a cappuccino, the Cortado. mixes velvety milk with a short, rich corto. extraction. It is two shots extracted for 10. seconds, with a textured milk. Our iconic Signature Blend is the perfect combination and balance of delicate Arabica and strong Robusta beans.
