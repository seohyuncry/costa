Beach Ball Shortcake Biscuit

It's a shortcake biscuit decorated with colored ponding icing. It is a dessert that is especially popular with children's customers.

Ingredients

Wheat Flour (WHEAT Flour, Calcium Carbonate, Iron, Niacin, Thiamin), Sugar, Glucose Syrup, Palm Oil, Unsalted Butter (MILK), Rapeseed Oil, Dried Free Range EGG White, Palm Stearin, Colours (Beetroot Red, Curcumin, Spirulina, Paprika), Flavourings, Raising Agents (Sodium Bicarbonate, E450(i)), Salt, Cornflour, Concentrates (Spirulina, Safflower), Stabiliser (Disodium Diphosphate), Concentrated Lemon Juice, Acidity Regulator (Acetic Acid).

! Adults need around 2000 kcal a day
