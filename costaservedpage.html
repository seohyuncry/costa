Our partners

Costa has a proven track record of delivering for its partners and customers. Founded in London in 1971 by the Costa brothers of Italy, Costa Coffee has a presence in 45 countries around the world, as well as 2,800 coffee shops in Britain and Ireland, and more than 1,100 coffee shops worldwide.
