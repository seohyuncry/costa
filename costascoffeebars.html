coffeebars

Our self-serve coffee bars

Bringing a sophisticated barista-style coffee experience to the outlet will attract more coffee-loving customers, which can generate additional revenue. Costa's earnings increased again in 2019. It reported a total of 1.34 billion pounds in revenue, up from 1.29 billion pounds a year earlier.

This is the world’s first integrated self-serve coffee machine, reinvented to deliver both hot and iced drinks. The machine can deliver over 500 barista-quality hot & iced drinks, from iced coffees to flavoured iced fruit cordials. The machines are being rolled out to over 40 Costa Express locations Summer 2021.

Introducing the all-new Marlow Ice

All-new Marlow Ice specifications

* More than double the drinks range. Over 500 barista style drink options.

* Iced drinks. Integrated ice delivery system with accurate dosing in every cup.

* Quick and easy to install. Only requires water, waste and power.

* Machine customisation. Include or exclude ice capability. Variety of cup sizes, payment options and syrup flavours are available.

* Coffee customisation. Marlow can deliver two different coffee blends to suit consumer tastes.

* Unique telemetry system. Proactive issue management, minimal downtime, detailed sales reporting.

* Easy to maintain. Just 30 minutes a day, minimising labour costs.

* Full support network. Preventative field maintenance and field-based quality control support.

  • Marlow

    Where footfall is high in your location, introduce our state-of-the-art Marlow coffee bar so that consumers who desire a premium Costa Coffee on the go, can enjoy the ultimate in barista quality self-serve convenience.

  • Marlow specifications

    * Crowd-pleasing smooth styling by Pininfarina.

    * Captivates all five senses with unique sound and smell elements.

    * 250+ drink combinations.

    * A fully modern and bright interactive HD touch screen.

    * Includes: Pay at Machine, Pay at Till, and Barcode Printer.

  • Compact

    Where space is a premium, the Compact delivers a quality Costa Coffee experience in a neatly designed machine. Its user-friendly touch screen makes it easy for consumers to enjoy their favourite drink from the classic Costa Coffee range.

  • Compact specifications

    * Instant Installation - all that’s needed is a 13 amp plug socket, water and waste connection, making it the perfect fit for retail outlets, convenience stores, petrol forecourts, motorway service areas and leisure facilities everywhere.

    * Space optimised design - fits into any space 2m high and 80cm wide, turning the smallest area into a profit generator, with a big increase in footfall.

    * Incredibly easy to restock and clean, taking staff only 30 minutes per day to maintain.

    * Offers smart support unique telemetry system which means we’re in constant contact so it’s easy for us to diagnose and fix any issues.

    * Serviced by a brand excellence team to ensure we always deliver the highest standards.

    * Available with a barcode printer.
