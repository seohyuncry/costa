OUR COFFEE

A faster way to free coffee

We combine our signature espresso with a creamy milk or dairy alternative of your choice for the ideal drink to sip when it's served hot or cold. Coffee making is an exact art, and at Costa Coffee we can make your favourite drink exactly as you like it. So, whether you prefer skimmed, full fat or soya milk, enjoy caffeinated, decaffeinated, or fair-trade coffee, the choice is all yours. We want to give our customers a wide range.
